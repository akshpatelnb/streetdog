Our Team


We’re working to get care, food and shelter for the stray animals of Gujarat, India. Dozens of animals of all kinds given the care they need to survive. But we’re not done. The streets of Gujarat in India are a rough place for stray animals, forced to fend for themselves against food scarcity, abuse, and temperatures up to 115 degrees. When I first saw the plight of these poor creatures, I knew I had to help. We formed our foundation to provide rescue crews, food, medical care, and training to give a brand new life to neglected dogs, cats, livestock and more. We’re thinking even bigger.
